I am now a post-doctoral fellow in [Multi-Media Lab (MMLab)](http://mmlab.ie.cuhk.edu.hk/) at The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK) and Centre for Perceptual and Interactive Intelligence (CPII), work with [Prof. Hongsheng Li](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=BN2Ze-QAAAAJ&hl=zh-TW&oi=ao). I recieved my Ph.D. Degree from Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences (CASIA) in 2020, advised by [Prof. Liang Wang](http://www.ia.cas.cn/sourcedb_ia_cas/cn/iaexpert/201010/t20101013_2986122.html), and my Bachelor and Master Degree from Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST) in 2014 and 2017, respectively. My current research interests include computer vision and machine learning.
